About Us

TR Poultry Farming

TR Poultry is a start-up company offering high-quality poultry and eggs, feed manufacturing, transportation, distribution and processing. We strive to provide customers with a high quality product, along with an unequaled commitment to service. We are a fully integrated poultry company, allowing us to give you superior poultry, straight from our farms to either your table or outlet venture.

We specialise in production of eggs, day old chicks, broilers, processed full chickens, and processed chicken portions.
