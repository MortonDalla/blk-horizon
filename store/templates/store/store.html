What We Do

Hospitality

Providing food services for functions, events, mobile food services and private/VIP dining experiences across Gauteng and Mpumalanga.

Mining Supplies

In 2018 the company further branch out into mining supplies and PPE apparel. Our business has also expanded to supplying sanitization/disinfectant services and sanitizer products over the year. The company currently employs 3 full time personnel.

Logistics

A key majority stake investment in ALL IN ONE Truckstop in 2018 based in Kendal Mpumalanga allowed inroads into the logistics sector providing truck and driver accommodation, mechanical services, trucking spares, truck washes, administration services, tyre repairs and sales. Recently All In One has gone mobile, providing the following services on the go:

  • Tyre Sales/ Repairs
  • Mobile Truck Wash
  • Service and Maintenance
  • 24/7 Breakdown Services
  • Truck Spares and Parts
  • Welding
  • Trailer Repairs
